Dosa?

A dosa is a thin, crispy crepe originating from South India. Made from fermented rice and lentil batter, it’s a staple in Indian households. Served with chutneys and sambar, dosa is a delightful combination of flavor and tradition.

Steps to Make the Perfect Dosa

Follow these simple steps to make a perfect dosa at home:

  1. Prepare the Batter: Soak rice and lentils, grind them into a fine paste, and ferment overnight.
  2. Heat the Pan: Use a flat, non-stick pan and ensure it’s evenly heated.
  3. Spread the Batter: Pour a ladle of batter in the center and spread it in a circular motion to create a thin layer.
  4. Cook and Serve: Drizzle oil around the edges, cook until golden brown, and serve hot with chutneys.
